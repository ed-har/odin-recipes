Vegetable Stew Recipe

Source: everydaydishes.com

Description

A vegetable stew, stripped into it's simplest form.
For when you need to increase your veggies intake, but are tight on the budget.

Ingredients

  • 1000 ml Water
  • 2 Carrots
  • Half Cabbagge
  • 200 gr Chickpea
  • 2 Potatoes
  • 9 tsp Chicken Bouillon Powder
  • Salt and Pepper - season to taste

Steps

  • Pour in the water and let it boil.
  • Put in the vegetables, cutted to your liking, and the chicken bouillon.
  • Turn down to medium heat and let the stew simmer for 15 minutes.
  • Taste the soup and veggies, season with salt and pepper or let it simmer for longer if needed.
  • Serve the dish and enjoy.
